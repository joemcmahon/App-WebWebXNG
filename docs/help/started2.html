Getting started

Navigating

The easiest way to navigate through a WebWeb is to click on whatever internal references appear on the pages you are viewing. You can also obtain a list of "all of the pages which reference the current document" by clicking on the view references icon:

(Use the "View Refs" link in the toolbar if icons are turned off.) Sometimes webs will have a search page which will allow you to search through all of the pages for a specific sub-string. You can also click on the search icon (or "Search All Pages" link, if icons are turned off):
